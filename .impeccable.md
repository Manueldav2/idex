# IDEX — Design Context

## Product
IDEX is a Chromium-shelled desktop app that hosts your coding agent (Claude Code, Codex, Freebuff) in a cockpit. While the agent generates, a picture-in-picture scroll feed slides in. When the agent finishes, the cockpit reclaims the screen.

## Audience
Developers who already live in a terminal. Specifically the taste-forward, TikTok-energy dev: young, online, opinionated, hates AI slop.

## Tone
Editorial. Magazine. Designer portfolio meets product site. Intentional, confident, unhurried. No jargon, no superlatives, no em-dashes, no "AI-powered" badges, no gradient text, no cyan/purple on dark backdrops.

## Direction
Refined minimalism with moments of editorial rhythm. Asymmetric layouts, generous whitespace, varied density, numbered sections. The landing should feel like the design budget was blown on it. Not optimized for clicks. Optimized for pause.

## Palette
Ink (near black with a blue undertone), paper (warm off-white), accent (electric blue, used sparingly). Neutrals tinted toward the blue. No pure black, no pure white.

## Typography
- Display: **Fraunces** variable (editorial, distinctive, humane)
- Body: **Manrope** (clean, modern, geometric)
- Mono: **JetBrains Mono** (code, timestamps, system chrome)

No Inter. No Roboto. No Open Sans. No system defaults.

## Motion
Strong ease-out curves, under 300ms for interactive. Scroll-driven morph is the headline animation. Everything else is restrained.

## Differentiation
The scroll-morph hero where a laptop screen physically turns into a phone while ambient hero words fly past. Nobody else has it. It's the one thing someone remembers.

## Writing rules
- No em-dashes. No en-dashes. Use commas, colons, periods, or line breaks.
- No marketing speak ("supercharge", "unlock", "revolutionize").
- Every word earns its place.
- Period at the end of short phrases reads more confident than exclamation points.
- Numbers written as numerals when they're counting things (3 agents, 12 cards).

## Anti-patterns
- AI illustrations (isometric robot brains, chat bubbles with sparkles)
- Glassmorphism over text
- Big rounded icons above section headings
- Identical card grids
- Hero metric templates (big number + small label)
